# Principle 7: Moiré is a Difference Amplifier

Moiré **magnifies tiny structural disagreements** between two sources into visible structures. A 0.5° rotation, a 1% scale mismatch, a sub-pixel shift — each becomes a macroscopic pattern.

This is what makes moiré useful as a *measurement instrument* (metrology, strain analysis) **and** as a generative medium: the pattern you see is the disagreement, blown up.
