# Mood to Structure Map

This map connects desired emotional qualities to specific moiré structures:

- **Hypnotic**: Low‑frequency radial moiré with slow phase drift creates a mesmerizing, entrancing effect.
- **Threatening**: Dense angular mismatches with sharp phase modulation produce aggressive, strobing interference.
- **Sacred**: Quasiperiodic grids combined with soft chromatic separation evoke a sense of hidden order and ritual.
- **Glitchy**: Sampling aliasing combined with hard thresholding gives a broken, digital aesthetic.
- **Biological**: Perturbed lattices with local curvature and noise fields mimic zebra stripes, leaf veins and other organic patterns.
