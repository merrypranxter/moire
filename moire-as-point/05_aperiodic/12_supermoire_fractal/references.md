# References

## Artists
- **Roman Verostko** — Algorithmic art with recursive structures. The visual ancestor of fractal moiré.
- **Manfred Mohr** — "P-197" and other algorithmic drawings. Systematic exploration of combinatorial pattern generation.
- **Vera Molnár** — "Des Ordres" (1974). Systematic variation of geometric parameters — the method behind supermoiré.

## Scientific
- **Twisted Trilayer Graphene** — "Supermoiré" in condensed matter physics. Nature Physics 2021. Fractal electronic bands from triple moiré interference.
- **Hofstadter's Butterfly** — Fractal energy spectrum of electrons in magnetic fields. The energy-level equivalent of supermoiré.
- **Cantor Set** — The original mathematical fractal. Self-similar structure at all scales — the abstract template for supermoiré.
- **Mandelbrot Set** — Complex fractal with infinite detail at all zoom levels. The mathematical sublime that supermoiré approximates.

## Historical
- **Gustav Hertz** — Early experiments with multiple interference gratings (1920s).
- **Optical Physics** — Multiple-beam interferometry. Each additional beam creates higher-order interference patterns.
- **Fourier Analysis** — The mathematical foundation. Any complex signal can be decomposed into sine waves; supermoiré is the visual synthesis.
