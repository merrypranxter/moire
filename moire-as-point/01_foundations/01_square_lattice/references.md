# References

## Artists
- **Takahiro Kurashima** — *Moiremotion* (2012). The definitive moiré art book. Spiral and kinetic moiré.
- **Gianni Sarcone** — Kinegram pioneer since 1997. Optical illusion engineering.
- **Rufus Butler Seder** — "Scanimation" books. Mass-market kinegram art.
- **Felice Varini** — Architectural anamorphic installations. Observer-dependent moiré at building scale.

## Scientific
- **Conoscopic Interferometry** — Fringe analysis for precision measurement. Same mathematics, scientific application.
- **Twisted Bilayer Graphene** — "Magic angle" physics: moiré superlattices create emergent electronic properties. Nature 2018.
- **Supermoiré in Trilayer Graphene** — Fractal moiré patterns in stacked 2D materials. Nature Physics 2021.

## Historical
- **Léon Bakst** — Early 20th century textile designs using moiré silk.
- **Victor Vasarely** — Op Art pioneer. Implicit moiré in geometric paintings.
- **Bridget Riley** — "Fall" (1963) and other works exploit interference-like perceptual effects.
