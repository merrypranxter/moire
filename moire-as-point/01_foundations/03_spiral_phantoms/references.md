# References

## Artists
- **Takahiro Kurashima** — *Moiremotion* (2012). The definitive work on spiral moiré in book form. Pages are spiral grids that animate as you flip.
- **Marina Apollonio** — "Dinamiche Circolari" series. Circular dynamic art with spiral-like motion effects.
- **Jesús Rafael Soto** — Venezuelan kinetic artist. "Penetrables" use layered linear elements that create moiré-like interference when viewer moves.

## Scientific
- **Galaxy Spiral Arms** — Density wave theory: spiral arms are interference patterns of stellar orbits, not fixed structures. The moiré of gravity.
- **Cyclotron Motion** — Charged particles in magnetic fields trace spirals. Interference between particles creates emergent patterns.
- **Phyllotaxis** — Plant growth spirals (sunflower seeds) follow Fibonacci/ golden angle patterns. Nature's moiré.

## Historical
- **Theodor Wolff** — 19th century spiral moiré experiments with rotating disks.
- **Max Wertheimer** — Gestalt psychology. Spiral moiré demonstrates "phi phenomenon" — apparent motion from static frames.
