# References

## Artists
- **Takahiro Kurashima** — *Moiremotion*. Extensive use of concentric ring moiré in page-based kinetic art.
- **Bridget Riley** — "Untitled (La Lune en Rodage)" (1963). Concentric circle paintings that exploit perceptual interference.
- **Marina Apollonio** — Italian Op Art. "Dinamiche Circolari" series uses concentric rings with moiré-like effects.

## Scientific
- **Conoscopic Holography** — Uses circular fringe patterns for 3D measurement. Same mathematics as radial moiré.
- **Bessel Beams** — Non-diffracting concentric rings in optics. The radial moiré analogue in physical light.
- **Acoustic Interferometry** — Circular interference patterns in sound waves. Direct physical analogue.

## Historical
- **Moiré Silk** — 18th century watered silk technique creates iridescent patterns through woven thread interference. The origin of the term "moiré."
