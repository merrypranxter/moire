# Variations

> Scaffold — fill in remix directions for this technique.

Suggested directions (from the concept):

1. **Clipping & quantization** — drive signals past range and celebrate the banding.
2. **Undersampling** — sample below Nyquist on purpose for aliased fringes.
3. **Threshold collapse** — push a soft signal through a hard threshold near the tipping point.
4. **Compression artefacts** — re-encode/decode and use the residue.
5. **Display-scaling errors** — render at one resolution, sample at another.
6. **Rolling shutter** — simulate a row-by-row capture against moving content.
